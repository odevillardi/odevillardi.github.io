A man from Okinawa, Japan planted a garden on the roof of his car, giving new meaning to the term ‘eco car.’

Because he didn’t have money to fix the air conditioner in his car, Mr. Chinen decided to plant seeds on the top of his car to grow plants in the hopes that they would make it a little cooler for him in the humid summer months. As his seeds sprouted, he noticed other random plants growing off his roof. It turns out that although he only planted sunflower seeds, everything else was either planted by passers-by or occurred naturally! (via Cool off your hot car with a rooftop garden)
